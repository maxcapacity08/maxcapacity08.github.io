The Scourge of the Empire - The Tale of Vraxxis, the Crimson Fang

Legends whisper of a shadow in the wilds, a force of nature born from blood and vengeance. Vraxxis, the Crimson Fang, is no ordinary beast—it is a fox-like predator with fur dark as the abyss and eyes that burn like molten gold. Clad in the scars of countless battles, its body moves like flowing fire, an omen of destruction to those who dare stand in its path.

Once, Vraxxis was no enemy of the Empire. It> was born in the sacred Sylvannoth Glades, where the spirits of nature wove harmony between man and beast. But the Emperor, in his insatiable thirst for dominion, set fire to the Glades, claiming the land as his own. The rivers ran black with ash, and the towering trees crumbled to dust. Vraxxis watched as its kin were hunted, their pelts worn as trophies by the Imperial elite.

On that day, the Crimson Fang was born.

Now, Vraxxis is a storm of vengeance, a nightmare that lurks just beyond the reach of torchlight. It slaughters imperial battalions with cunning ambushes, its claws sharper than steel, its fangs drenched in the blood of oppressors. No walls can hold it, no army can subdue it. Whispers spread through the Empire—stories of an unstoppable hunter who rends flesh from bone, who moves unseen through the night, and who will not rest until the Emperor himself drowns in his own tyranny.

Some say it is merely a beast, driven by rage. Others believe Vraxxis has grown beyond the mortal realm, now wielding the forgotten magic of the Sylvannoth. The Empire calls it a demon. The oppressed call it salvation.
